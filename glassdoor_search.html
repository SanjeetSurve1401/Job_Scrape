      QA Test Engineer (Manual) – 1 to 3 Years

      Job Description

      Balewadi, Pune | Work From Office 

      We are hiring a QA Test Engineer (Manual) with 1–3 years of experience in software testing. The candidate should have strong knowledge of testing concepts and hands-on experience in executing test cases and defect tracking.

      Key Responsibilities:

      Prepare & execute test cases based on requirements
      Perform Functional, Regression & Smoke testing
      Log and track defects using Jira
      Coordinate with developers for bug resolution
      Support UAT & release validation as per the project need

      Required Skills: 

      1–3 years of Manual Testing experience
      Good understanding of SDLC & STLC
      Experience with Jira or similar tools
      Basic SQL knowledge (preferred)
      Familiarity with Agile methodology

      Good to Have: 

      Experience in API testing using Postman.
      Exposure to working on live client projects.
      Understanding of release management process.

      Why Join Us: 

      Work on real-time client projects.
      Structured growth opportunities.
      Collaborative and professional work environment.
      Exposure to end-to-end QA lifecycle

      Pay: ₹300,000.00 - ₹400,000.00 per year

      Benefits:

      • Health insurance
      • Provident Fund

      Work Location: In person

      Base pay range

      The minimum salary is ₹3L and the max salary is ₹4L.
